PB12. LO 6.6 — Elm Connections

Question Using the adjusted trial balance, compute net sales, gross margin, gross profit %, and prepare simple and multi-step income statements.
Quick solution: Net Sales = Sales − discounts − returns; Gross Margin = Net Sales − COGS; Gross Profit % = GM / Net Sales; then build simple and multi-step IS with operating vs other items.
